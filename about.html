About Us

EaseRun was founded by runners who knew the struggle of choosing between expensive comfort and painful, affordable shoes. We experienced the blisters and aches firsthand and believed no one should have to compromise. That's why we created EaseRun, a brand dedicated to delivering exceptional comfort and support for every runner. We use innovative, purpose-built materials to make running feel softer and easier. By selling directly to you, we provide this premium comfort at a radically affordable price. We built the shoes we always needed, so you can focus on the joy of the run.
